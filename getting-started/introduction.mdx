ACME Company is a powerful platform designed to help teams collaborate, manage projects, and achieve their goals efficiently. Whether you're a small startup or a large enterprise, our tools scale with your needs.

## What is ACME Company?

ACME Company provides a comprehensive solution for modern teams that need to:

- **Collaborate effectively** with real-time editing and commenting
- **Manage projects** with intuitive dashboards and tracking
- **Integrate seamlessly** with your existing workflow tools
- **Scale confidently** with enterprise-grade security and performance
- **Analyze performance** with detailed insights and reporting

## Who uses ACME Company?

Our platform serves diverse teams across industries:

- **Product teams** building and launching new features
- **Engineering teams** collaborating on complex projects
- **Marketing teams** coordinating campaigns and content
- **Sales teams** tracking prospects and managing relationships
- **Customer success teams** ensuring client satisfaction

## Key features

### Team Collaboration

- **Real-time collaboration** - Work together seamlessly with live editing and instant updates
- **Smart notifications** - Stay informed without being overwhelmed
- **Comments and reviews** - Provide feedback directly in context
- **Version history** - Track changes and revert when needed

<Callout kind="tip">
  Enable desktop notifications to stay updated on important changes without constantly checking the app.
</Callout>

### Project Management

- **Visual project boards** - Organize work with customizable Kanban boards
- **Advanced filtering** - Find exactly what you need with powerful search
- **Custom workflows** - Adapt the platform to your team's process
- **Timeline tracking** - Monitor progress and identify bottlenecks

### Integrations

Connect with the tools you already use:

- **GitHub/GitLab** - Sync code repositories and pull requests
- **Slack/Teams** - Get notifications in your chat workspace  
- **Jira/Linear** - Link issues and track development progress
- **Figma/Adobe** - Embed designs and prototypes
- **Google Workspace** - Share documents and calendars seamlessly

### Security & Compliance

- **Enterprise SSO** - Single sign-on with SAML/OAuth support
- **Role-based permissions** - Control access with granular permissions
- **Audit logs** - Track all user activity for compliance
- **Data encryption** - Your data is protected at rest and in transit
- **SOC 2 Type II** - Independently verified security controls
